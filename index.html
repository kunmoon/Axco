Welcome to AxCo! We are a team of expert consultants who specialize in providing guidance and advice to organizations on how to make their products, services, and environments more accessible to people with disabilities. At our company, we have a deep understanding of the unique needs of individuals with disabilities and the various ways in which these needs can be accommodated. We have expertise in a wide range of fields, including web design, software development, architecture, and engineering. This allows us to provide a comprehensive range of services to our clients. Our team of accessibility consultants can help organizations of all sizes and in all industries to meet the requirements of various laws and regulations that mandate accessibility for people with disabilities. We can provide services such as conducting accessibility audits, providing training on accessibility best practices, and offering recommendations for improving the accessibility of a website or other digital tool. By working with us, organizations can ensure that their products and services are usable and accessible to all individuals, regardless of their abilities. This can help to improve the customer experience, increase brand loyalty, and avoid costly lawsuits and other legal issues. We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our services and how we can help your organization become more accessible.
