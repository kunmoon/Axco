Accessibility Consulting

Welcome to our accessibility consulting website! We are a team of expert consultants who specialize in providing guidance and advice to organizations on how to make their products, services, and environments more accessible to people with disabilities.

At our company, we have a deep understanding of the unique needs of individuals with disabilities and the various ways in which these needs can be accommodated. We have expertise in a wide range of fields, including web design, software development, architecture, and engineering. This allows us to provide a comprehensive range of services to our clients.

Our Services

  • Conducting accessibility audits
  • Providing training on accessibility best practices
  • Offering recommendations for improving the accessibility of a website or other digital tool
